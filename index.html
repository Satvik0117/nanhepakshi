Discover

We started with a single small scale project with the idea of free basic education for all with now organizing many big scale projects with core focus on three sectors namely education, hygiene and personality development of an individual.

Suraksha

Suraksha as the name suggests focuses on healthy danger free living of an individual. The project is a tie up with Delhi Police and Delhi Traffic Police. It organizes self defense classes in collaboration with Delhi Police and helps Delhi Traffic Police synchronize Delhi's traffic for a better and danger free movement.

Unnati

Unnati focuses more on different social issues in the society and works upon to correct them. This project is mainly lead by our Volunteers and Interns. Under this project we groom not a person but together a society. Different donation campaigns, water aid drives, cleanliness drives, health check ups, blood donation camps and many more all run under the project Unnati.
